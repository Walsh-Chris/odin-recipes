Punch Chicken

Easy 10 minute Tasty Chicken Dish

Want a quick and simple dish for the whole family? Then this punch chicken recipe is the recipe for you. With just a few Ingredients this chicken dish will be in the oven and cooking.

Ingredients required

  • 500g skinless chicken breast
  • 100g thinly sliced ham or bacon
  • Jar of salsa of preferred heat level
  • 200g grated cheese
  • Salt and pepper to season

Step

  1. Preheat oven to 180c or 160c for fan forced for 15mins
  2. Lightly grease an oven tray or use baking paper
  3. Place each chicken breast on tray
  4. Using fist or rolling pin punch/ flatten the chicken out to 1cm thickness
  5. Season chicken liberally with salt and pepper
  6. Open salsa and pour onto chicken breast, ensure coverage
  7. Lay strips of bacon or ham on top
  8. Sprinkle grated cheese on top of chicken breasts
  9. Put chicken in the oven and bake for 25mins or until cheese is golden brown and chicken is fully cooked
