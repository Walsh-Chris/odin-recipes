Tacos

Family favorite taco tuesday

Looking for a quick and easy meal thats sure to satisfy the whole family? Look no further then tacos

Ingredients required

  • 500g minced beef,chick or pork
  • Head of lettuce
  • 2 Ripe tomatoes
  • 10 Hard corn taco shells
  • Taco seasoning
  • Sour cream
  • Taco Sauce
  • 1tbsp Olive oil or spray oil

Step

  1. Preheat oven to 180c or 160c for fan forced for 15mins
  2. Place pan on stove on high heat
  3. Spray pan with oil or add 1tbsp olive oil
  4. Place in mince and cook until fully browned and drain off the water
  5. Open and empty sachet of taco seasoning onto the mine and a cup of water then mix in
  6. Lower the temp and let the mince simmer for 5mins
  7. While mince is simmering cube the tomatoes and dice the lettuce
  8. Place taco shells on oven tray and cook for 10mins in oven
  9. Once taco shells and mince is ready its time to plate
  10. Everyone makes there tacos differently so have fun and don't forget the sour cream
