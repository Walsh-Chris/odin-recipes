Fairy Bread

Kids Favorite

Kids hungry for a snack or your hosting a birthday party then this recipe i for you.

Ingredients required

  • 1 loaf of white bread
  • 1 tub of butter or margarine
  • 1 jar of multi-coloured sprinkles

Steps

  1. Butter 4 or more slices of white bread
  2. Sprinkles the aptly named sprinkles onto the buttered bread
  3. cut the bread into triangles
  4. Arrange bread triangles on plate standing up for good presentation
  5. Let the kids and the big kids hook in
